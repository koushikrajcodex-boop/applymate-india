# Student Testing Plan

This plan is for checking whether ApplyMate India eligibility filters are understandable and accurate for real students.

## Goal

Find out whether students can use the scholarship finder without confusion and whether the recommended matches look reasonable for their profile.

## Suggested testers

Start with 5 to 10 students:

- 2 Andhra Pradesh students
- 2 Telangana students
- 2 national-scholarship seekers
- At least 2 engineering or technical-course students
- At least 2 students from category/special groups such as SC, ST, OBC, EWS, minority, girls, or disability support

## Test script

Ask each student to complete these tasks:

1. Open the scholarship finder.
2. Select state, education, category, and income range.
3. Search for one keyword related to their case, such as engineering, girl, minority, AP, Telangana, NSP, or AICTE.
4. Pick the top 3 results that look relevant.
5. Open official links for those results.
6. Say whether each result is accurate, confusing, or not relevant.
7. Try the document planner and roadmap planner.
8. Explain what they would change before trusting the result.

## Feedback questions

Use these questions:

- Did the filter options match your real student profile?
- Were any important categories missing?
- Did any scholarship appear that you clearly were not eligible for?
- Was the income range easy to understand?
- Did the result explain enough before sending you to the official portal?
- What would make you trust the result more?
- Which page confused you the most?

## Accuracy scoring

For each tested result, mark:

- `Accurate`: eligibility appears correct after checking official source.
- `Needs review`: result may be useful but needs clearer notes.
- `Incorrect`: result should not appear for that profile.
- `Unknown`: official source was unclear or unavailable.

## Success target

Before calling the filter reliable, aim for:

- At least 80% of tested results marked `Accurate` or `Needs review`.
- Zero high-confidence incorrect results for state, category, or income.
- At least 5 real student profiles tested.

## Follow-up

Turn repeated problems into GitHub issues using the Scholarship correction template.
