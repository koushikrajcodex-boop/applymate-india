This directory lists important scholarship schemes for Indian students across national, state, technical education, merit, category, gender, disability, and special-group support.

Use this page as a starting point. Scholarship deadlines, eligibility rules, income limits, and document requirements can change. Always verify final details on the official portal before applying.
