AP Jnanabhumi Scholarship Guide

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Jnanabhumi is commonly used by Andhra Pradesh students for scholarship-related services, especially post-matric support. Students should carefully verify their category, institution, course, income limit, and document requirements.

Who should check Jnanabhumi?

  • Intermediate students in Andhra Pradesh
  • Degree, engineering, and PG students
  • Students from eligible welfare categories
  • Students whose college participates in the official scholarship process

Common documents needed

  • Aadhaar card
  • Income certificate
  • Caste/category certificate if applicable
  • Bank passbook
  • Previous marks memo
  • College bonafide certificate
  • Attendance proof if required

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Using old income certificate
  • Wrong bank details
  • Mismatch in Aadhaar or student name
  • Not checking college verification status
  • Assuming rules are same for every category

Official portal

Always verify details here: Jnanabhumi Official Portal.

FAQ

Does ApplyMate India guarantee scholarship approval?

No. It only shows possible matches and document guidance.

Should I verify on Jnanabhumi?

Yes. Always verify final details on the official Jnanabhumi portal.
