Scholarships for Girl Students in India

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Girl students may be eligible for special scholarships that support higher education, technical education, merit-based study, or specific categories. These schemes can reduce financial pressure and encourage continued education.

What to check before applying

  • Whether the scholarship is only for girl students
  • Course type and institution approval
  • Family income limit
  • Number of eligible children per family, if mentioned
  • Required documents
  • Application deadline

Common documents

  • Aadhaar card
  • Admission proof
  • Income certificate
  • Previous marks memo
  • Bank passbook
  • Bonafide certificate

FAQ

Are girl student scholarships only for engineering?

No. Some are for technical education, while others may support school, degree, or other higher education.

Should I verify official rules?

Yes. Always verify on the official portal before applying.
