Scholarships for Girl Students in India

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Girl students may be eligible for special scholarships that support higher education, technical education, professional courses, merit-based study, or category-based financial assistance.

Eligibility differs by scheme. Always verify final rules, deadlines, and documents on the official portal.

Who should check girl student scholarships?

  • Girl students studying school, intermediate, degree, diploma, engineering, or PG courses
  • Students applying for technical education scholarships
  • Students from eligible income or category groups
  • Students looking for merit-based or state-based support

What to check before applying

  • Whether the scholarship is only for girl students
  • Course type and institution approval
  • Family income limit
  • Number of eligible children per family, if mentioned
  • Required documents
  • Application deadline

Common documents

  • Aadhaar card
  • Admission proof
  • Income certificate
  • Previous marks memo
  • Bank passbook
  • Bonafide certificate
  • Category certificate if applicable

Common mistakes

  • Not checking whether the course is eligible
  • Missing the official deadline
  • Uploading unclear documents
  • Entering incorrect bank details
  • Assuming all girl students automatically qualify

FAQ

Are girl student scholarships only for engineering?

No. Some scholarships are for technical education, while others may support school, degree, or other higher education.

Can category-based scholarships also apply to girl students?

Yes, depending on the scheme rules. Some scholarships may consider both gender and category/income eligibility.

Should I verify official rules?

Yes. Always verify on the official portal before applying.
