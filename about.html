About ApplyMate India

ApplyMate India is a simple scholarship finder web app created to help Indian students quickly discover possible scholarship opportunities, required documents, eligibility notes, and official apply links.

Many students miss scholarship opportunities because information is scattered across different portals. ApplyMate India tries to make the first step easier by showing possible matches based on state, education level, category, and family income.

What this app helps with

  • Finding possible scholarship matches
  • Checking basic eligibility information
  • Viewing document checklists
  • Opening official application portals
  • Reading beginner-friendly scholarship guides
  • Sharing useful scholarship information with others

Important note

ApplyMate India is not an official government website. Scholarship rules, deadlines, income limits, and required documents can change. Always verify final details on the official portal before applying.
