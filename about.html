About ApplyMate India

Last updated: 26 June 2026

ApplyMate India is a free student-help website created to make scholarship discovery easier for Indian students. Many students miss scholarship opportunities because information is spread across different portals, notices, and official websites. ApplyMate India tries to simplify the first step by showing possible scholarship matches, document checklists, guide pages, and official verification links.

Important: ApplyMate India is not an official government portal and does not collect or submit scholarship applications. Students must always verify final details on the official scholarship portal.

What ApplyMate India helps with

  • Finding possible scholarships based on state, education level, category, and income range
  • Understanding common documents needed for scholarship applications
  • Reading beginner-friendly guides for NSP, AP Jnanabhumi, Telangana ePASS, and other student topics
  • Accessing official portal links for final verification
  • Reporting incorrect or outdated information for correction

What ApplyMate India does not do

  • It does not guarantee scholarship approval or selection
  • It does not replace official portals
  • It does not submit applications on behalf of students
  • It does not ask for payment to show scholarship information
  • It does not store student form selections on a server in the current version

How information is handled

Scholarship information is simplified for student awareness. Since rules, deadlines, income limits, and documents can change, ApplyMate India uses source notes, last verified dates, disclaimers, and correction links to improve transparency.

To understand how information is selected and reviewed, read the Editorial Policy.

Why this project was created

This project was created to help students quickly understand where to start. Scholarship portals can sometimes feel confusing for beginners, especially when students are not sure which documents are needed or which portal applies to them. ApplyMate India gives a simple starting point before students visit the official website.

Contact and corrections

If you find incorrect scholarship information or want to suggest a scholarship to add, please use the Contact page.

ApplyMate India is an informational tool. Scholarship rules, income limits, deadlines, and required documents may change. Always verify on the official portal before applying.
