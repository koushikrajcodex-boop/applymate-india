Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

ApplyMate India respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how this website handles information, form selections, external links, advertising, cookies, analytics, and contact messages.

Information we collect

The current version of ApplyMate India does not require login, registration, password creation, or personal account creation. Students can use the scholarship finder without creating an account.

Information entered in the scholarship finder

When users select details such as state, education level, category, income range, or search keywords, the website uses those selections inside the browser to show possible scholarship matches. In the current version, ApplyMate India does not store these selections on a server.

Email contact

If users contact ApplyMate India by email, the email address and message content will be visible to the website creator for the purpose of replying, reviewing corrections, checking scholarship suggestions, or improving the website.

Advertising and cookies

ApplyMate India may display advertisements through Google AdSense or other advertising partners in the future. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's visits to this website or other websites.

Cookies may help advertising partners show relevant ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and improve ad quality. Users can manage cookies through their browser settings. Users may also manage ad personalization through Google ad settings where available.

Analytics

ApplyMate India may use analytics tools in the future to understand basic website usage, such as page views, popular pages, device type, and general traffic sources. If analytics are added, this Privacy Policy may be updated with more details.

External links

ApplyMate India links to official scholarship portals and other external websites. External websites have their own privacy policies and terms. ApplyMate India is not responsible for the content, privacy practices, security, or availability of external websites.

Children and students

ApplyMate India is designed as an informational student-help website. Students should use official portals carefully and avoid sharing sensitive personal information anywhere unless they are sure the website is official, secure, and relevant to their scholarship application.

Data storage

ApplyMate India currently works as a static website. The scholarship finder runs in the browser and does not store eligibility form selections on a server in the current version.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated when new features are added, such as analytics, contact forms, advertising, user accounts, or other services.
