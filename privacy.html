Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

ApplyMate India respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how this website handles information.

Information we collect

The current version of ApplyMate India does not require login, registration, or personal account creation. The eligibility form works in your browser and is used only to show possible scholarship matches.

Information you enter

When you select details such as state, education level, category, and income range, the app uses those selections to filter scholarship results. This information is not stored by ApplyMate India in the current version.

Advertising and cookies

ApplyMate India may display advertisements through Google AdSense or other advertising partners in the future. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's visits to this website or other websites.

These cookies may help advertising partners show relevant ads, measure ad performance, and improve ad quality. Users can manage cookies and ad personalization through their browser settings or Google ad settings.

External links

ApplyMate India may include links to official scholarship portals and other external websites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of external websites.

Data storage

ApplyMate India currently does not store user form selections on a server. The scholarship finder runs in the browser using static website files.

Future updates

If ApplyMate India adds analytics, ads, forms, or user accounts, this Privacy Policy may be updated to explain what data is collected and how it is used.
