Documents Needed for Scholarship Applications

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Scholarship applications often require documents to prove identity, education, income, category, and bank details. The exact list changes by scheme, so always verify on the official portal.

Common document checklist

  • Aadhaar card or Aadhaar enrolment details
  • Income certificate
  • Caste/category certificate if applicable
  • Disability certificate if applicable
  • Bank passbook or bank account details
  • Previous marks memo
  • School or college bonafide certificate
  • Admission proof
  • Fee receipt if required
  • Passport-size photograph

Tips before uploading documents

  • Make sure photos/scans are clear.
  • Check spelling of name and Aadhaar details.
  • Use valid certificates.
  • Keep file size and format as required by the official portal.
  • Do not wait until the last date.

FAQ

Is income certificate always needed?

Many scholarships need it, but not all. Check the specific scheme guidelines.

Can unclear documents cause rejection?

Yes. Blurry or incorrect documents may delay or affect verification.
