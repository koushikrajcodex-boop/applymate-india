How scoring works

The planner gives extra weight to urgent deadlines, stronger eligibility, ready documents, and higher benefit. It subtracts a little for high effort.

  • Deadline: closer valid deadlines get more urgency points.
  • Eligibility: strong matches rank higher than uncertain options.
  • Documents: ready documents increase practical chance of applying on time.
  • Benefit: higher-value scholarships move up the list.
