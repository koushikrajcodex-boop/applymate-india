Scholarship help for students in India

ApplyMate India is built for students who want a quick starting point before applying for scholarships. You can filter by state, education level, category, and income range. The app also shows common document checklists, source notes, and official portal links so students can verify details directly.

Students can create an optional account to save scholarships, complete a student profile, and track application status in a personal dashboard.

This website is not an official scholarship portal. Always confirm eligibility, deadlines, income limits, and documents on the official government or scholarship website before applying.

How results are selected

ApplyMate India compares your selected state, education level, category, income range, and search keyword with scholarship entries stored in the website.

Each result may include an eligibility note, income note, common document checklist, official source link, last verified date, and source note.
