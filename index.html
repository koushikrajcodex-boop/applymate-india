Scholarship help for students in India

ApplyMate India is built for students who want a quick starting point before applying for scholarships. You can filter by state, education level, category, special group, income range, and keyword.

The app shows possible scholarship matches with eligibility notes, income notes, document checklists, official source links, and last verified information. Students can also create a free account to save scholarships and track application status.

This website is not an official scholarship portal. Always confirm eligibility, deadlines, income limits, required documents, verification steps, and payment updates on the official government or scholarship website.

What you can do with ApplyMate India

  • Find possible scholarships based on your selected details.
  • Search scholarships by keyword such as engineering, girls, minority, SC, ST, OBC, disability, AP, Telangana, AICTE, or NSP.
  • View common document checklists before applying.
  • Open official source links for final verification.
  • Save scholarships to apply later after login.
  • Track application status in your dashboard.

FAQs

Is ApplyMate India an official scholarship portal?

No. ApplyMate India is not an official scholarship portal. It is an informational student helper. Students should always verify final eligibility, deadlines, income limits, and required documents on official portals.

Can ApplyMate India show scholarships based on my details?

Yes. ApplyMate India can show possible scholarship matches based on selected details such as state, education level, category, income range, and search keyword.

Can students save and track scholarships on ApplyMate India?

Yes. Students can create an account, save scholarships, and track application status in the ApplyMate India dashboard.

ApplyMate India is an informational tool. Scholarship rules, income limits, deadlines, and required documents may change. Always verify on the official portal before applying.
