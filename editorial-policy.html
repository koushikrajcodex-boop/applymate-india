Editorial Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

ApplyMate India aims to simplify scholarship information for students. The website is created as an informational tool and is not an official government or scholarship portal.

How information is selected

Scholarship information is added based on publicly available official portals, government scholarship websites, state scholarship portals, and reliable student-support sources.

How information is written

ApplyMate India explains scholarship details in simple language so that students can understand the first steps before visiting the official application portal.

Verification approach

Each scholarship card includes a source note and last verified date where possible. However, scholarship rules, deadlines, income limits, and documents can change at any time.

Corrections

If users find outdated or incorrect information, they can report it by email. Corrections should include the scholarship name, official source link, and suggested correction.

Important reminder

Students should always verify final details on the official portal before applying. ApplyMate India does not guarantee eligibility, approval, selection, or scholarship payment.

ApplyMate India is an informational tool. Always verify details on official portals before applying.
