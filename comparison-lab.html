How to choose smarter

Start with eligibility

Do not waste time on schemes that do not match your course, category, gender, disability status, or income range.

Check documents early

Income, caste/category, bonafide, marks memo, and bank details often take time to arrange.

Prefer official clarity

A scheme with a clear official source and deadline is safer than a vague scholarship listing.
