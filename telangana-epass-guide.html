Telangana ePASS Scholarship Guide

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Telangana ePASS is used for various student welfare scholarship applications. Students should check their category, education level, income eligibility, college details, and document requirements before applying.

This guide helps Telangana students understand basic preparation, common documents, verification steps, status tracking, and mistakes to avoid before using the official ePASS portal.

ApplyMate India is not an official Telangana ePASS portal. Always verify final information, deadlines, income limits, documents, and status on the official website.

Who should check Telangana ePASS?

  • Intermediate students in Telangana
  • Degree, engineering, diploma, and postgraduate students
  • Students from eligible welfare categories
  • Students applying for post-matric scholarship support
  • Students who need to track scholarship application or payment status

Common documents needed

  • Aadhaar card
  • Income certificate
  • Caste or category certificate
  • SSC or 10th marks memo
  • Previous qualifying exam marks memo
  • Bank passbook or bank account details
  • College bonafide certificate
  • Admission proof if required
  • Passport-size photo if required

Application checks

  1. Check whether your category is eligible.
  2. Verify the income limit on the official portal.
  3. Confirm your course and college details.
  4. Keep clear scanned documents ready.
  5. Submit details carefully before the deadline.
  6. Track application and verification status regularly.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Entering wrong caste or category details
  • Using expired or incorrect income certificate
  • Entering wrong bank account or IFSC details
  • Missing college verification
  • Uploading unclear document images
  • Waiting until the last date

Status tracking tips

After applying, students should check whether the application is submitted, pending at college, verified, approved, rejected, or under payment processing. If the application is pending at college, contact your institution scholarship section.

Official portal

Verify final details here: Telangana ePASS Official Portal.

FAQ

Can Telangana engineering students use ePASS?

Eligible engineering students may need to check Telangana ePASS depending on scheme rules.

Can income limits change?

Yes. Always verify current income rules on the official ePASS portal.

Is ApplyMate India final proof of eligibility?

No. ApplyMate India is only a student awareness and planning tool.
