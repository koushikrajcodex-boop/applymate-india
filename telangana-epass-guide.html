Telangana ePASS Scholarship Guide

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Telangana ePASS is used for various student welfare scholarship applications. Students should check their category, education level, income eligibility, college details, and document requirements before applying.

ApplyMate India is not an official Telangana ePASS portal. Always verify final information on the official website.

Who should check Telangana ePASS?

  • Intermediate students in Telangana
  • Degree, engineering, diploma, and PG students
  • Students from eligible welfare categories
  • Students applying for post-matric scholarship support
  • Students who need to track scholarship application status

Common documents needed

  • Aadhaar card
  • Income certificate
  • Caste/category certificate
  • SSC or 10th marks memo
  • Previous qualifying exam marks memo
  • Bank passbook
  • College bonafide certificate
  • Passport-size photo

Application checks

  1. Check whether your category is eligible.
  2. Verify income limit on the official portal.
  3. Confirm your course and college details.
  4. Keep clear scanned documents ready.
  5. Submit carefully before the deadline.
  6. Track application and verification status.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Wrong caste/category details
  • Expired or incorrect income certificate
  • Wrong bank details
  • Missing college verification
  • Uploading unclear document images

Official portal

Verify final details here: Telangana ePASS Official Portal.

FAQ

Can Telangana engineering students use ePASS?

Eligible engineering students may need to check Telangana ePASS depending on scheme rules.

Can income limits change?

Yes. Always verify current income rules on the official ePASS portal.

Is ApplyMate India final proof of eligibility?

No. It is only a student awareness tool.

ApplyMate India is an informational tool. Always verify details on official portals before applying.
