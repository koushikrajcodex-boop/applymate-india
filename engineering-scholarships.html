Engineering Scholarships in India

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Engineering students may be eligible for different types of scholarships depending on state, category, gender, income, disability status, merit, institution approval, and course type.

Types of scholarships to check

  • State post-matric scholarships
  • National Scholarship Portal schemes
  • AICTE-related scholarships
  • Girl student scholarships
  • Disability scholarships
  • SC/ST/OBC/EBC category scholarships
  • Private merit scholarships

Documents commonly needed

  • Aadhaar card
  • Admission proof
  • Previous marks memo
  • Income certificate
  • Category certificate if applicable
  • Bank passbook
  • Bonafide certificate

Before applying

Check whether your course, college, admission year, and institution approval status are accepted by the scholarship scheme.

FAQ

Can private college students apply?

Sometimes yes, but it depends on the scheme and institution eligibility.

Is income certificate always required?

Many scholarships require it, but rules vary by scheme.
